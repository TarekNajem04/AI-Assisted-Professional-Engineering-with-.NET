# AI-Assisted Professional Engineering with .NET
# Project Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap provides a high-level view of the project's current status, planned milestones, and long-term direction.

The roadmap is intentionally conservative.

Items listed here represent planned work, not commitments. Priorities may evolve as the book matures, community feedback is collected, and new insights emerge during the writing process.

---

Current efforts include:

- Export workflows
- Release planning
- Community participation guidelines

# Planned

## Book Development

The book will be developed incrementally through a section-by-section publishing model.

Planned work includes:

- Chapter authoring
- Technical review
- Diagram creation
- Sample development
- Translation review
- Publication review

---

# Publication Strategy

The project follows an incremental publication approach.

Publication units:

- Section
- Chapter
- Release

Each published section becomes part of the evolving manuscript and contributes to future chapter and book releases.

---

# Community Feedback Phase

During the early stages of the project, community feedback will be used to evaluate:

- Topic relevance
- Reader interest
- Technical depth
- Publishing cadence
- Documentation quality

Feedback collected during this phase may influence future priorities.

# Long-Term Vision

The project aims to become a practical and professional reference for engineers working with AI-assisted software development in modern .NET environments.

The focus remains on:

- Engineering discipline
- Architecture
- Maintainability
- Reproducibility
- Professional software craftsmanship
- Responsible use of AI tooling
