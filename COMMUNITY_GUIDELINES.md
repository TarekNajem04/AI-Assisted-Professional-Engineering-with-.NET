# Community Guidelines

Welcome to the AI-Assisted Professional Engineering with .NET community.

This project is built around the idea of sharing knowledge, encouraging professional discussion, and exploring how Artificial Intelligence can be integrated into modern software engineering practices.

To help maintain a constructive and valuable environment, please follow the guidelines below.

---

# Be Respectful and Professional

Treat all participants with professionalism and respect.

Healthy technical disagreement is encouraged.

Personal attacks, hostile behavior, harassment, or disrespectful communication are not acceptable.

Assume good intentions and focus discussions on ideas rather than individuals.

---

# Focus on Learning

The primary purpose of this community is education and professional growth.

Contributions should aim to:

* Share knowledge
* Improve understanding
* Encourage thoughtful discussion
* Help readers learn from different perspectives

Winning arguments is less important than improving collective understanding.

---

# Stay Relevant to the Project

Discussions should generally relate to topics covered by the book, including:

* Software Architecture
* .NET Engineering
* AI-Assisted Development
* Engineering Workflows
* Software Quality
* Testing
* Operations
* Governance
* Maintainability
* Professional Engineering Practices

Broader technology discussions are welcome when they contribute meaningfully to these topics.

---

# Ask High-Quality Questions

When seeking help or clarification:

* Provide sufficient context.
* Explain what you are trying to understand.
* Reference the relevant chapter or section when possible.
* Include examples when appropriate.

Clear questions lead to better discussions.

---

# Share Constructive Feedback

Feedback on the manuscript is highly encouraged.

Useful feedback often includes:

* Technical corrections
* Clarification requests
* Alternative viewpoints
* Missing considerations
* Suggestions for improvement

Criticism is welcome when it is specific, respectful, and actionable.

---

# Respect the Publication Process

The manuscript is published incrementally.

Some topics may be intentionally incomplete while future sections are being prepared.

Please review the Roadmap and Project Status documents before opening issues related to planned content.

---

# AI Discussions Require Critical Thinking

The project encourages thoughtful use of AI.

Avoid treating AI-generated output as automatically correct.

When discussing AI-assisted engineering practices:

* Question assumptions
* Validate claims
* Share evidence
* Consider trade-offs

Engineering judgment remains essential.

---

# Collaborate Generously

Community members are encouraged to:

* Share experiences
* Provide examples
* Recommend resources
* Help answer questions
* Contribute to constructive discussions

A strong community grows through shared learning.

---

# Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers may:

* Organize discussions
* Reclassify issues
* Close duplicate topics
* Remove inappropriate content
* Moderate conversations when necessary

Moderation decisions are intended to preserve a productive and professional environment.

---

# Community Values

This community values:

* Professionalism
* Curiosity
* Humility
* Evidence-based discussion
* Continuous learning
* Long-term thinking

These values reflect the broader principles that guide the project.

---

# Thank You

Thank you for contributing your time, experience, and ideas.

Every thoughtful question, discussion, correction, and insight helps improve both the manuscript and the community surrounding it.
